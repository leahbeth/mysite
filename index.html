About Me

Alpine mountaineering in the French Alps

I'm a bilingual (French/English) Technical Product Manager passionate about leveraging technology to address climate challenges. With experience spanning from Syngenta to innovative startups, I specialize in building products that deliver both business value and environmental impact.
