About Me

Bridging Technology and Environmental Impact

I'm a bilingual (French/English) Technical Product Manager passionate about leveraging technology to address climate challenges. With experience spanning from Syngenta to innovative startups, I specialize in building products that deliver both business value and environmental impact.

Currently engaged with CDR.fyi's NBS portal and participating in Cohort 8 of the Climatebase Fellowship, I'm deeply involved in carbon removal technology and methodology development. My work focuses on critiquing and improving carbon credit frameworks while developing practical solutions for enterprise-level environmental challenges.

Based between Boston and the French Alps, I bring a global perspective to product development, combining technical expertise with deep domain knowledge in agricultural technology, geospatial data, and environmental modeling.
